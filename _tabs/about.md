## Why write?

**Writing forces clarity**

I can't pretend I understand something until I can explain it simply. Every post here is me wrestling a concept to the ground until it submits. If it's on this blog, I've actually sat with it long enough to have an opinion worth writing down.

Also - the internet gave me everything I know. Every research paper someone explained clearly, every ML concept someone broke down without gatekeeping, every "I was confused about this too" blog post from someone three steps ahead of me. This is my way of paying that forward.

> *"The best way to learn something is to build it, break it, then write about it."*
